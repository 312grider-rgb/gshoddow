Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
This is a starting template, not legal advice. Have a lawyer review and customize this before you rely on it for a real launch — requirements vary by country, and if you serve users under 13, additional laws (like COPPA) apply.

1. What We Collect

When you use Paradise Of The Eternal Learners, we collect:

  • Account information: name, email, password (encrypted), and role (student or teacher)
  • Profile details you add: bio, avatar image
  • Learning activity: courses viewed, lessons completed, certificates earned, saved courses, and reviews you write
  • Messages you send through the Contact form or AI Tutor

2. How We Use It

We use your data to operate your account, track your learning progress, issue certificates, personalize AI recommendations, and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. AI Features

Questions you ask the AI Tutor, along with relevant lesson context, are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This data is used to produce your answer and is subject to Anthropic's own data handling terms.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database provider. We apply access controls (row-level security) so that only you can see your own private data, such as your progress and saved courses.

5. Your Rights

You can view and edit your profile information at any time from your Profile page. To request full account deletion or a copy of your data, use the Contact page.

6. Children's Privacy

Our academic courses include content designed for younger learners (Kindergarten through High School), but account creation currently assumes a user capable of consenting to these terms. If your platform will knowingly serve children under 13 with their own accounts, additional protections (such as COPPA-compliant consent flows) are legally required — this should be addressed before launch.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our Contact page.
